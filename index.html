Use and limitations

📌
Personal operational aide-mémoire
Not an official Royal Ascot publication
Use alongside the event fire-risk assessment, site plans, local orders, briefings and supervisor direction.
Do not replace a qualified fire-risk assessor, fire engineer, event safety officer or competent person.
Do not calculate occupancy, approve alterations or sign off compliance unless formally authorised and competent.
Record and escalate any matter outside your role or authority.

The app stores tick boxes, notes and patrol details locally in your browser. Nothing is transmitted anywhere.
