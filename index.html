Use and limitations

This is a personal operational aide-mémoire, not an official Royal Ascot publication or formal fire-risk assessment.

Use it alongside the event fire-risk assessment, local instructions, site plans, briefings and supervisor direction.

Do not calculate occupancy, approve alterations, reset systems or certify compliance unless formally authorised and competent.

Observe, take immediate action within your authority, record and escalate.
